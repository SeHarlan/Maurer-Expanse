Guide

Press "r" to randomize your starting point (the top left pattern or "Base Location")


Once you've found an interesting location, use arrow keys to step through and explore the Maurer Expanse.

If you click on a pattern and hold you can move directly there.

Arrow keys are disabled while this menu is open.

Use "-" and "+" to zoom out and in.

Zooming out a lot can increase rendering time

Use "[" and "]" to decrease or increase the depth (amount of change between patterns)

Changing the depth by doubling or halving leads to the most recognizable changes

Once you've set the perfect scene, use "s" to save a screenshot or "p" to render a high quality version and save that.


If you would like to start over and reset all the base parameters use "q"
